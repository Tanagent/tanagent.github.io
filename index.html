Projects

Foond
(Android)

Android application that helps user figure out what restaurant they would like to go to. This app allow users to upload food images to the database (AWS) and have it be associates with a restaurant where that food images is located (Yelp API and Google Maps API). It then displays all the images on the main menu where users are able to see all the images that are uploaded, thus giving them an idea of which restaurant they would like to go to.

Minglr
(Android)

Android application that was submitted at HackPoly 2017 competition and was developed in a team of 4 in 24 hours working span. The app solves the problem of having to eat a restaurant by yourself and having to go through a lot of small talk before coming up with a date and plan. This app goes straight to the point and displays the restaurant and time that you are available and displays it to all users. Other users can view the other person's profile and confirm that a date can be made.

vTrainer
(Android)

It is your own personal virtual trainer on your Android device. Users set the amount of time that they would like to work out for and vTrainer does the rest. Users are allowed to select their own exercises and vTrainer randomly generate those exercises along with rest time in between each exercise. This app was inspired by the idea of personal trainers and how clients would pay hundreds of dollars for someone to tell them what to do. Instead of having someone to tell you, why not have an Android app that does all that for you?
